The Method

How We Score

01

The Pour

Two-part pour, proper angle, patience between pours. The barkeep's craft is everything. No shortcuts tolerated.

02

The Taste

Creamy, balanced, with that distinct roasted bitterness. Served at the right temperature — cold but never ice-cold.

03

The Head

Thick, tight, and domed. It should hold the shape of a shamrock if drawn. Anything less is negligence.

04

The Venue

Atmosphere matters. Clean lines, proper glassware, staff who understand the craft. A great pint deserves a great setting.
